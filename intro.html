Diverse London

Visualize the urban diversity

"Urban Diversity", as an important urban issue, is always discussed in different contexts, such as ethnic diversity and mixed land use. To some extent, the diversity of cities also reflects the inclusiveness of different groups or cultures. London has long been known for its diversity, and the government has also been committed to it. Do you want to know what a diverse city London is?
